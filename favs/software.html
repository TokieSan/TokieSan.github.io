Tokhy's Software

Introduction

Here is the software I use.

LineageOS, Crdroid

The Android Custom ROMs I use. I prefer non-google flavored setups, so I use either LineageOS or Crdroid with MicroG.

Arch Linux

The GNU/ Linux distribution I use, find more about it in my philosophies. I am emotionally attached to Pacman, and the AUR

DWM

The Dynamic Window Manager I use.

Vim

My 90%-of-time IDE and favourite text editor. Check my .vimrc

Vscodium

In the projects that require many testing/ implementations, I go for Vscodium as an IDE, with Zen mode and Vim emulator extension.

KdenLive

The video editor I use. Thanks to KDE team for their contributions to the open source community.

GIMP

The Image Manipulator I use, it needs to be a bit flavored then it will be ready to go for almost all of my needs.

Audacity

The Audio editor I prefer.

Links, Firefox

The browsers I use. While I do not agree with many of the shit done by Firefox, when it is configured it can be able to suit my needs for a modern web client for webapps/ college/ work stuff. However, links is my go-to runner for any simple web purpose.

Yay

Say whatever you want, this helper is cool af, but always remember to check your diffs.

FOSS Droid

My source for Android Apps.

Jellyfin + Kodi

Jellyfin is my media server software, one of my favs of the favs in this list and I use all the time. I use Kodi as a client in my TV and Laptop with Embuary skin that offers many cool features as holiday suggestions.

Whoogle

While I do not like google, I have to admit that they ripped enough data of its users that the results are the best out there of its evil competitors. Also, I do not trust DuckDuckGo. Thus, I decided to host a whoogle instance so I can search as safely as possible.

Slide

The reddit browser client I use. While I hate reddit as a company and website, I use it sometimes to fill my void or catch up with life when I am sick of 4chan, Discord, and games.

NewPipe

The Youtube client I use. It is full of amazing features, and allows me to visit youtube with the least possible ass-tracking by the spyware google.

FFmpeg, MPV, and VLC

Media shit I use. FFmpeg is the most popular backend for everything that I use to manipulate video/audio with simple commands that I scraped off StackOverFlow. MPV is my media client I use on my personal computers/ laptops besides Kodi. VLC is my go-to for android because of cool features as the implemented subtitle search engine.

Subscene

The website I use for subtitles. However, their cloudflare shit is screwing up all of my addons so I may consider switching to OpenSubtitles.

KeePassXC, KeePassDx

Password managers. They rock. Thanks to the glorious KeePass

KDE Connect

Thanks again to KDE for their magnificant-ecosystem-facilitator app.

Xbindkeys

My fav keybindings here

PiHole

The DNS Sinkhole I use.

LibreOffice

Best office suite there!

Spotify MPD & Cantata

Quitted Spotify and went for MPD and Cantata client for music. Visit this blogpost for more info.

Ubuntu DejaVu Fonts

This is my favorite font-family, I use DejaVu Sans Mono for my IDEs and Sans version for my WM. DejaVu Sans Mono is the first preference here. However, Importing the font will be a bloat so if you have it installed then cool. If not, the site is only fucking text anyways!

Also, I am dyslexic, and DejaVu Sans Mono is the easiest-but-still-beautiful font to read to me.
