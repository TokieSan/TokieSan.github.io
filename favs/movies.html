Introduction

Movies are for movie nights and emotion dealing; they're independent of your past and future, only a present to have for a specific situation. I do not watch much movies, but here is the one only movie that deserved to be put in this page. Also, to expand, I'll include my favorite director.

12 Angry Men

I do not remember any details of the day I watched this movie. However, I still remember each second of it. I remember each word by all of the actors. When movies go for the "flexing" to attract customers, this movie was only shot in a small room, with only unique ideas and moving acting. This drama went beyond the limits, it affected my life positively, maybe changed my perspective on many things. I'm usually a man who gives no shit about the rating, but this can be the only movie in the "top-rated movies" list that was underrated. Even the first won't be enough for this piece of art.

Wes Anderson

He is not here due to his gorgeous skills, he is here because he is the only director 'similar' to me. I was recommended his movie "The Grand Budapest Hotel" by a friend. When I watched it, it was literally everything I was thinking of doing in movies. His style, music, editing, etc. interactively presents me. If you want to know more of "me" go watch this legend's movies.
